**Employee Performance Evaluation**
The company has approximately 500 employees across more than 60 positions. A digital solution needs to be proposed for evaluations during or at the end of the probation period, before contract extension, and for periodic performance reviews of employees on permanent contracts. The system should support different forms and criteria depending on the position or group of positions, deadlines, self-assessment, manager evaluation, comments, development goals, training recommendations, and decisions regarding contract extension.
